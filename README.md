---

## 🧪 Engineering the Glow (Core Features)

### 🧠 **Fuzzy Logic Engine**
Unlike traditional binary "Good/Bad" apps, SkinLogic speaks the nuanced language of dermatology using **Fuzzy Logic**. It eliminates uncertainty by calculating personalized compatibility scores through mathematical **Membership Functions**.

* **Impact:** Instead of saying "Alcohol is bad," it reasons: *"Contains low-grade alcohol; given your oily skin profile, the irritation risk is only 20%."*

### 💎 **Routine Synergy & Harmony**
Skin-care is about chemistry. SkinLogic analyzes how ingredients interact across your entire routine. It identifies conflicts—like the irritation risk of mixing **Vitamin C** with **Retinol**—before you even apply them.

### 📸 **Deep Scan OCR & NLP**
Utilizing high-precision Optical Character Recognition, it transforms complex Latin INCI (International Nomenclature Cosmetic Ingredient) lists into actionable data sets, normalizing terms against a 400+ item database.

### 🕵️‍♂️ **Explainable AI (Transparent Engineering)**
We don't just provide a score. The "Why this score?" feature breaks down the logic, showing exactly how much each ingredient contributed to hydration, soothing, or irritation.

## 📈 Engineering Challenges & Solutions

* **Dynamic Data Orchestration:** Migrating from static code-base storage to a relational **Supabase** architecture to optimize JS thread performance and ensure real-time data updates.
* **OCR Noise Mitigation:** Implementing NLP-driven normalization to match "noisy" camera-captured text with standardized chemical names with 95%+ accuracy.
* **Modular Architecture:** Maintaining strict separation between the **Fuzzy Logic Engine** and the **UI components** to ensure scalability and ease of testing.
